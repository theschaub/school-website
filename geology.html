Geology Overview

If you have always wanted to know more about the wonders of our planet and how humans go about using Earths resources, this course is for you. This course is an introduction to physical geology and will examine Earths rocks and minerals, Earths geological and biological history, the formation and structure of rocks, plate tectonic theory, weathering and erosion processes. Geology 12 is a course designed to familiarize students with the geologic world. Students will be given the opportunity to apply their knowledge of geologic processes to interpret geologic situations from both a global and local perspective. Students will learn to understand the world around us: developing our international mindedness and giving them the opportunity to better understand our community, and environment. Throughout the course every effort will be made to show how Science connects to other areas of study and life in general. Students will be encouraged to communicate their ideas and consider the thoughts and opinions of others. Students will also work to develop themselves as communicating, caring, inquiring, risk taking, knowledgeable, reflective, open-minded, principled, balanced, and thinking individuals.
